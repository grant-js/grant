# Business Model Recommendations

## Executive Summary

Your open-core SaaS model is **well-structured** and follows proven patterns. Here are strategic recommendations to strengthen the business model.

## 🎯 Strategic Recommendations

### 1. **Pricing Strategy Refinement**

#### Current Pricing

- Starter: $99/month (100 users)
- Professional: $299/month (1,000 users)
- Enterprise: Custom

#### Recommendations

**Add Usage-Based Tier**:

- Consider a "Pay-as-you-go" tier for startups
- $0 base + $0.50 per active user/month
- Attracts early-stage companies
- Natural upgrade path to Starter

**Annual Discounts**:

- Offer 20% discount for annual payments
- Improves cash flow and reduces churn
- Common industry practice

**Usage Limits**:

- Starter: 100 users, 10 organizations
- Professional: 1,000 users, 100 organizations
- Enterprise: Unlimited
- Clear upgrade triggers

### 2. **Feature Gating Strategy**

#### Critical: Define "Core" vs "Enterprise"

**Must Stay Open Source** (Core):

- Basic RBAC/ACL functionality
- User, role, permission management
- Organization and project scoping
- Basic API endpoints
- Self-hosting capabilities

**Can Be Enterprise-Only**:

- Advanced analytics dashboards
- SSO integrations (SAML, LDAP, AD)
- SCIM provisioning
- Custom branding/white-labeling
- Advanced compliance (SOC2, HIPAA)
- Premium support tooling
- Advanced audit logging
- Custom integrations

**Recommendation**: Be **very clear** about what's free vs paid. Ambiguity hurts trust.

**Developer Experience**:

**Metrics to Track**:

- GitHub stars (target: 1,000 in Year 1)
- npm downloads (target: 10K/month in Year 1)

- Documentation page views

#### Phase 2 Enhancements (SaaS Beta)

**Beta Program**:

- [ ] Recruit 10-20 beta customers

- [ ] Collect detailed feedback
- [ ] Build case studies

**Feature Development Priority**:

1. **SSO Integration** (highest demand)
2. **Advanced Analytics** (differentiator)

### 4. **Revenue Optimization**

#### Additional Revenue Streams

**Marketplace/Integrations**:

- Charge for premium integrations (e.g., Salesforce, ServiceNow)
- Revenue share with integration partners
- White-label integration marketplace

**Professional Services**:

- Implementation services: $5,000-$50,000
- Custom development: $150-$250/hour
- Training workshops: $2,000-$5,000/day

**Support Tiers**:

- Community: Free (GitHub, Discord)
- Standard: Included in Starter/Professional
- Premium: $500/month add-on (4-hour response)
- Enterprise: Included (1-hour response, dedicated manager)

### 5. **Competitive Positioning**

#### Differentiation Points

**vs. Auth0/Okta**:

- ✅ Open-source core (no vendor lock-in)
- ✅ Self-hosting option
- ✅ More affordable
- ✅ Better developer experience

**vs. Keycloak**:

- ✅ Modern architecture (TypeScript, GraphQL)
- ✅ Better developer experience
- ✅ Managed SaaS option
- ✅ Better documentation

**vs. Custom Solutions**:

- ✅ Faster time-to-market
- ✅ Battle-tested security
- ✅ Ongoing maintenance included
- ✅ Community support

### 6. **Risk Mitigation**

#### Potential Risks

**Risk: Forking**

- **Mitigation**: Keep core valuable, make enterprise features compelling
- **Reality**: Most forks don't maintain long-term

**Risk: Competitors**

- **Mitigation**: Focus on developer experience and community
- **Reality**: First-mover advantage in open-core RBAC space

**Risk: Pricing Pressure**

- **Mitigation**: Flexible pricing, annual discounts
- **Reality**: Value-based pricing (not cost-based)

## 🎯 Success Criteria

### Product-Market Fit Indicators

- 10+ paying customers
- 3+ case studies
- <5% monthly churn
- NPS > 50
- Organic growth > 30% month-over-month

### Scale Indicators

- $50K+ MRR
- 50+ paying customers
- 5+ enterprise customers
- Positive unit economics (LTV:CAC > 3:1)
